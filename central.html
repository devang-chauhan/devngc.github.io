Internal Company Website to Organize Information and Automate Quality Check

Between 2014 and 2018, I worked at MSA in India as a senior architect and sustainability architect. While I worked on several projects during these four years, I observed that a lot of information that should have been accessible to all the employees was not. This led to inefficiency and inconsistency in project delivery. As a solution to that, I created this internal website that hosted updated information on items of regular use.

Design technologist

Having the ability to iteratively develop solutions, workflows, products, and businesses is the reason why I got interested in technology.

I have been in the positions of an architect, a project manager, a consultant, a sustainability specialist, a computational designer, and a software developer.

If you asked me, I would say that it has been a great career progression. In all these roles, I found myself drawn to building processes and systems to capture human intelligence.

In the world of consulting, time is money. In 2016, I learned to code out of curiosity to develop a system to automate some parts of the job I was doing then in India. I have authored technology in all of the jobs since to reduce time in delivering value.

In 2021, amid the covid pandemic, I got an opportunity to join a startup and automate the job I was doing at the time. I took the plunge.

Talk to me to find out what happened next and how I see life.

Script repository

At HOK, as a team of computational design leaders we observed that every office has a lot of smart people that create time-saving workflows in the rush of the projects that other people at other offices don't know about. This creates a situation where the human intelligence of an international firm is isolated and often remains unknown to people working in distant offices. We prototyped a platform that can enable everyone to share their workflows. The goal of this repository was to ultimately help the firm offer smart Design Analytics.

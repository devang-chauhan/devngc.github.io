Daylight Factor

A ratio of inside illuminance to outside illuminance.

The following factors affect daylight factor;

  • Building geometry
  • Surrounding obstructions
  • Optical surface properties

The limitation of this metric is that it does not consider facade orientation, season, time of the day, direct sun ingress, and programmatic use
